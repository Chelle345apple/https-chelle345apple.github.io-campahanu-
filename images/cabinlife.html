Cabin Life 101

Inside the Cabins:

You'll share your cabin with 7-9 other campers and a friendly counselor who's there to make sure everyone is safe, having fun, and feels included.

Do I Get to Choose My Bunk?

Bunks are chosen by a first come, first serve basis. That means that the camper who arrives first, gets the first pick. Campers can trade bunks after all campers have arrived. Your camp counselor can help you negotiate things with your fellow campers, but there is no guarantee that you will get the bunk you prefer.

What Should I pack for Camp?

Camp Ahanu Packing List

Bedding and Sleep Essentials

  • Twin-size fitted sheet, flat sheet, pillows with pillow cases, and a light blanket OR sleeping bag
  • Stuffed animal or comfort item (optional)
  • Flashlight or headlamp with extra batteries
  • Earplugs or sleep mask (if needed)

Clothing

  • 6-8 T-shirts
  • 4-6 pairs of shorts
  • 2-3 pairs of long pants or leggings or jeans for hikes in the woods
  • 6-8 pairs of shorts
  • 1 lightweight jacket or raincoat
  • 7-10 pairs of socks
  • 7-9 pairs of undergarments
  • 2-3 sets of pajamas
  • 2 swimsuits
  • 1 pair of flip flops
  • 1 pair of sneakers or closed-toe shoes for active play
  • 1 pair of sandals or water shoes with a strap

Toiletries

  • Toothbrush, toothpaste, mouthwash, floss
  • Hairbrush or comb
  • Shampoo and conditioner
  • Soap or body wash
  • Deodorant
  • Sunscreen (non-aerosol)
  • Bug spray (non-aerosol)
  • 2 sets of Towels and washcloths
  • Shower caddy or toiletry bag

Camp Gear

  • Water bottle labeled with name
  • Backpack or daypack
  • Laundry bag
  • Books or journal and pen or pencil
  • Stationary/stamps if writing letters home
  • Reusable plastic bags or web bag for dirty/wet clothes
  • Hat and sunglasses
  • Camp-appropriate costume or fun outfit (for theme days)

Optional Items

  • Favorite snacks (non-perishable)
  • Camera (no phones or iPads)
  • Playing cards or travel games
  • Small fan (battery opperated)
  • Extra battereis
  • Swim googles
  • Musical instrument (if applicable)

Can I bring my cellphone, laptop, iPad, Switch, or Smart device?

Cell phones, iPads, laptops, game devices, and smart watches are not allowed at camp. To call home during free time, campers may use the rotary phone in the Camp office.
