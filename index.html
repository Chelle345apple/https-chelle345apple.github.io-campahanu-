About Camp Ahanu

Camp Ahanu is a fun, safe, and creative place for kids to explore nature, make friends, and enjoy unforgettable adventures

Our mission is to create a safe, vibrant, and inclusive space where kids can explore new intersts, discover the joy of movement and wellness, and build lifelong friendships. Through hands-on science adventures, scouting and camping skills, creative activities, and fun challenges, we empower every camper to grow with confidence, curiosity, and heart - all while having the time of their lives.

At Camp Ahanu, our motto is "Adventure. Friendship. Discovery."
